Whisper Fine-Tuning Jobs

Whisper fine-tuning specialists customize OpenAI's speech model for domain-specific applications, achieving 20-40% accuracy improvements over vanilla Whisper.

Companies are discovering that while Whisper is powerful out-of-the-box, fine-tuning on domain data (medical terminology, legal jargon, technical vocabulary) dramatically improves accuracy. These specialized roles focus on dataset preparation, transfer learning, and evaluation for production deployments.

What Whisper Fine-Tuning Engineers Do

  • Dataset Curation: Collect and annotate domain-specific audio (medical consultations, legal depositions)
  • Transfer Learning: Fine-tune Whisper on 10-100 hours of custom data
  • Evaluation: Measure WER improvements on domain-specific test sets
  • Prompt Engineering: Optimize initial prompts for better domain accuracy
  • Production Integration: Deploy fine-tuned models alongside base Whisper

Common Industries Hiring

  • Healthcare: Medical documentation platforms (Abridge, Suki, DeepScribe)
  • Legal Tech: Court transcription, deposition services (Verbit, Rev)
  • Finance: Earnings call transcription, compliance monitoring
  • Enterprise SaaS: Meeting tools with industry-specific vocabulary
