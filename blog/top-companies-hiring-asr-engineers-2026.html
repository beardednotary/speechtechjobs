How to Choose the Right Company

Use this decision framework:

If You Value: Compensation Above All

Top choices: OpenAI, Meta, Google, Anthropic

These pay $300K-$500K+ TC at senior levels.

If You Value: Cutting-Edge Research

Top choices: OpenAI, Meta FAIR, DeepMind, Anthropic

Publication-friendly, access to massive compute.

If You Value: Startup Equity Upside

Top choices: AssemblyAI, Deepgram, early-stage startups

Higher risk, but 0.5-1% at a $500M company = potential millions.

If You Value: Work-Life Balance

Top choices: Microsoft, Apple, Nuance, Cisco

40-45 hour weeks, less pressure, better boundaries.

If You Value: Product Impact

Top choices: Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, OpenAI

Billions of users actually using your work.

If You Value: Ownership & Autonomy

Top choices: Series A/B startups, smaller teams

More responsibility, less structure.

Red Flags to Watch For

When evaluating offers, watch out for:

  • Vague equity terms: Ask for exact shares, not percentages
  • "We'll revisit comp in 6 months": They won't
  • No other speech engineers: You'll be building from scratch (could be good or bad)
  • Funding concerns: Check runway for startups
  • High attrition: Check Glassdoor and back-channel
  • Pivoting companies: Speech might not be core focus

The Application Process

Timeline for these companies:

  • FAANG: 4-8 weeks, 5-6 interview rounds
  • Startups: 2-4 weeks, 3-4 rounds
  • Enterprise: 3-6 weeks, 4-5 rounds

Pro tip: Apply to 3-5 companies simultaneously to create leverage in negotiations.

The Bottom Line

The best company for you depends on:

  1. Your career stage (early career vs. senior)
  2. Your priorities (money, research, impact, balance)
  3. Your risk tolerance (FAANG safe vs. startup equity)
  4. Your location preferences (office, remote, hybrid)

General recommendations:

  • 0-2 years experience: Target Google, Meta, or well-funded Series B+ startups for best learning
  • 3-5 years experience: Choose based on what you want next—research, leadership, or specialization
  • 6+ years experience: Go where you can have maximum impact and comp
